Purpose of this site

This site describes some of the benefits of Agile Project Management Methodologies and why they may be beneficial to your next software development project.

If you are already familiar with the processes and want to supplement your experience with an official Scrum Master Certification we explore the differences between the Certified Scrum Master (CSM) and Professional Scrum Master (PSM) to help you decide which one to persue.
